About Us

Who We Are

PKSB is a vibrant community organization representing people of Pakistani origin in Braunschweig, Germany. We serve as a bridge between cultures, fostering connection and understanding.

Our Mission

To create a welcoming space where Pakistani culture thrives while promoting integration and intercultural exchange in Braunschweig.

Our Goals

  • Promote Pakistani culture and traditions
  • Support newcomers with integration
  • Encourage intercultural dialogue
  • Build a strong, connected community
